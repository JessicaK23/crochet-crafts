Hello everyone, and welcome to Crochet Crafts. I am delighted to have the opportunity to show case my crochet craft pieces with you. Crochet is very dear to my heart and every piece I crochet is made with the upmost love care and joy.

I have crocheted since I was very young. However since the Pandemic (Covid 2019), crochet has now become a true hobby of mine for the last four years. I am so looking forward to sharing my back story and many of the creations I have made with you. I hope you will all enjoy them as much as I have anjoyed making them.
